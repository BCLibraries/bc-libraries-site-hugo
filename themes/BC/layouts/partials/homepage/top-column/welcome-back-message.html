Welcome Back! We are providing a full suite of services (with some modifications, of course). Research consultations, instruction classes, and reference services are online. Nearly all Course Reserves are online. To meet CDC and Massachusetts guidelines, there are building access requirements, reduced seating, hours, and public technology. Because masks should remain on, all libraries will have a "no food" policy, although most will still allow drinks. Check it out — the 5th floor study area in O'Neill was enlarged to provide more seating overlooking downtown Boston!

For the latest information, consult the BC Libraries' homepage and the BC Libraries Updates page.
