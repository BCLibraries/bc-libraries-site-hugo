Welcome Back! We are ready to provide a full suite of services (with some modifications, of course). Research consultations, instruction classes, and reference services will be online. Nearly all Course Reserves will be online. To meet CDC and Massachusetts guidelines, there is reduced seating, hours, and public technology. Because masks should remain on, all libraries will have a "no food" policy, although most will still allow drinks. Check it out — the 5th floor study area in O'Neill was enlarged to provide more seating overlooking downtown Boston!

For the latest information, consult the BC Libraries' homepage and the BC Libraries Updates page.
